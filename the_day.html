The Ceremony

On the day, the ceremony will start promptly at 4pm at The Asylum. We suggest that you arrive from 3:30pm so you are ready and seated.

The ceremony will last approximately 20 minutes and be followed by a drinks reception.

The Dinner

At 6pm a coach will take us in two groups to the East Dulwich Tavern.

Dinner will be served there at around 7pm. If you're joining us for dinner please let us know if you have any special dietry requirements as soon as possible.

Following dinner will be coffee, cake and speeches.

The Evening

Evening drinks and DJ will start at 9pm at the same venue.

Carriages

The celebrations will finish upstairs at midnight. However, the downstairs part of the pub is open until 1am should you wish to make the evening last a touch longer!

Dress Code

Wear whatever you'll feel comfortable in.
