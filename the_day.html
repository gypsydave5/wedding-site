The Ceremony

On the day, the ceremony will start promptly at 4pm. We suggest that you arrive from 3:30pm so you are ready and seated.

The ceremony will last approximately 20 minutes and be followed by a champagne reception.

The Reception

At 6pm we will move to the reception venue, where dinner will be served around 7pm.

The Evening

If you've been invited to the evening only, please arrive at the reception venue at 9pm or after.

Following dinner will be speeches, cake and a DJ. The celebrations will finish upstairs at midnight. However, the downstairs part of the pub is open until 1am should you wish to make the evening last a touch longer!

Dress Code

Wear whatever you'll feel comfortable in.
