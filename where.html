Ceremony

We're getting married at The Asylum, a deconsecrated chapel turned arts venue:

Caroline Gardens Chapel
Asylum Road
SE15 2SQ

Public transport

The nearest station is Queens Road Peckham. This station is served by Southern Trains (out of London Bridge) and London Overground (the line between Clapham Junction and Highbury & Islington). The venue is about a 5-10 walk from the station down Asylum Road. There are also a number of buses which serve the area.

By car

The venue has no specific parking, so we recommend public transport where possible. However there is some on street parking in the area.
